About Me

Hello! I'm Diya Patel, a passionate and creative UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with a strong focus on crafting beautiful, user-centric digital experiences. With a keen eye for design and a passion for technology, I specialize in creating seamless web and mobile interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive to use.

I have experience working with a variety of design and development tools, including Figma, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and more. My journey in the tech world began with an interest in how design and functionality can come together to create an impactful user experience. Over time, this interest evolved into a commitment to continuously learn and adapt to the ever-changing world of web development and design.

Outside of work, I enjoy experimenting with new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, and collaborating with other developers and designers. My goal is to not only build efficient and attractive websites but also to stay ahead of the curve in UI/UX design, ensuring that every project I work on provides a meaningful and accessible user experience.
